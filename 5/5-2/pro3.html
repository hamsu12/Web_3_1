IEEE 802.11ac and IEEE 802.11ax

Brief introduction

IEEE 802.11ac

IEEE 802.11ac is amendment to IEEE 802.11, published in December 2013, that build on 802.11n. The 802.ac standard was retroactively labelled ac Wi-Fi Alliance.

IEEE 802.11ac

IEEE 802.11ac is a successor to 802.11ac, marketed as Wi-Fi 6 (2.4GHz and 5GHz) by the Wi-Fi Alliance. It is also known as High Effiency Wi-Fi, for the overall improvements to Wi-Fi 6 clients under dense environments.
